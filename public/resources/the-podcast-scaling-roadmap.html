The 5 Scaling Obstacles That Kill Growth

Obstacle #1: Time Scarcity Trap

You're spending 80% of your time on production and only 20% on growth activities. This keeps you busy but not growing.

Solution: Flip the ratio. Successful podcasters spend 20% on content, 80% on amplification and growth.

Obstacle #2: Single Platform Dependency

You're only on Apple Podcasts or Spotify. Your growth is limited by one platform's algorithm and discovery mechanisms.

Solution: Become omnipresent. Your podcast should fuel content for 6+ platforms simultaneously.

Obstacle #3: Audience vs. Community Confusion

You have listeners, but not a community. Audiences consume. Communities engage, share, and buy.

Solution: Create feedback loops. Ask questions, respond to comments, build relationships.

Obstacle #4: Revenue Ceiling Mindset

You think podcasts only make money through ads or sponsorships. This limits your earning potential to someone else's budget.

Solution: Your podcast is a lead generation and authority-building machine. The real money is in what it enables.

Obstacle #5: Manual Everything Syndrome

You're personally doing every task: editing, posting, responding, promoting. This doesn't scale.

Solution: Build systems and delegate operations. Your job is strategy and relationships, not tasks.
