You're a Time-Starved Podcaster

Diagnosis: You're spending too much time on content creation instead of growing your audience.

What's Happening

  • • You're doing everything yourself
  • • Editing takes hours each week
  • • No time left for actual growth activities
  • • Posting is inconsistent or rushed
  • • You're burning out on content creation

Quick Fixes

  • • Batch record 2-3 episodes at once
  • • Use templates for consistent posting
  • • Focus on 2-3 platforms max
  • • Create content calendars in advance
  • • Consider automation tools

Your Action Plan

Week 1

Stop doing everything yourself. Identify the 3 most time-consuming tasks and find ways to automate or delegate them.

Week 2

Create templates for your social media posts and captions. This alone will save you hours.

Week 3

Batch create 4 weeks of content in one session. Schedule everything in advance.

Week 4

Use the time you saved to actually engage with your audience and network with other creators.

You're the Invisible Expert

Diagnosis: Your content is good but no one is seeing or engaging with it.

What's Happening

  • • Great content but terrible reach
  • • Posts get few likes or comments
  • • Algorithm isn't showing your content
  • • Wrong timing or platforms
  • • Content doesn't hook viewers quickly

Quick Fixes

  • • Start with stronger hooks in first 3 seconds
  • • Post when your audience is most active
  • • Use trending hashtags in your niche
  • • Create cliffhanger-style clips
  • • Engage back when people comment

Hook Formulas That Work

"The mistake that cost me $50,000..." - Start with consequences

"Here's what nobody tells you about..." - Promise inside knowledge

"I tried [X] for 30 days. Here's what happened..." - Story-based hooks

"If you're struggling with [X], stop doing this..." - Problem-solution format

You're the Scattered Strategist

Diagnosis: You're doing a lot of things but without a clear strategy or focus.

What's Happening

  • • Posting on too many platforms
  • • No clear content strategy
  • • Inconsistent messaging and branding
  • • Don't know what content works
  • • Spinning wheels without direction

Quick Fixes

  • • Pick 2-3 platforms and focus there
  • • Define your niche and stick to it
  • • Create content pillars (3-4 topics)
  • • Track what content gets engagement
  • • Develop a consistent posting schedule

Your Focus Framework

Step 1: Choose Your Core Message

What's the ONE thing you want to be known for?

Step 2: Pick Your Power Platforms

Where does your ideal audience spend time? Focus there.

Step 3: Create Content Pillars

3-4 topics you'll consistently cover every month.

Step 4: Track and Double Down

What gets the most engagement? Do more of that.
