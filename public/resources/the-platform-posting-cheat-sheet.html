Platform Quick Reference

Each platform has its own rules. Here's what works best for podcast content on every major social media platform.

Instagram

Post Frequency: 1-2 times daily

TikTok

Best Time: 6 PM - 10 PM

Post Frequency: 1-3 times daily

YouTube

Post Frequency: 3-5 times weekly

LinkedIn

Best Time: 8 AM - 10 AM
Top Format: Professional Tips
Post Frequency: 3-5 times weekly

Twitter/X

Post Frequency: 3-5 times daily

Facebook

Best Time: 1 PM - 3 PM

Post Frequency: 3-4 times weekly
